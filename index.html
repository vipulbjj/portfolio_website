Principles

Operating beliefs — stable even when the venture changes.

  • Uncertainty is the object. Markets, models, and minds are studied for what they cannot guarantee — not for illusory certainty.
  • Claims must face friction. A hypothesis earns belief only after data, users, or an adversarial market pushes back.
  • Companies are experiments. Startups are how I run studies papers cannot — the product may pivot; the method does not.
  • Implementation is part of the proof. From HFT to shipped ML: if it cannot be built and measured, it is not finished research.
  • Structure before scale. LLMs help judgment when memory, synthesis, and explicit uncertainty exist; they harm it when prompts replace thinking.
  • Write the failures. Weekly essays are a public lab notebook — including what did not work.
